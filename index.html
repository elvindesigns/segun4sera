Meet Ebozoje Deogratias Segun

A digital innovation consultant and community advocate, Segun brings a new vision of smart, transparent, and inclusive estate leadership.

His focus is on harnessing technology, collaboration, and transparency to create a more connected Sun City, one where residents’ voices shape real outcomes.

Vision

A Shared Vision for the Future

My vision is to make Sun City a model of modern estate living — efficient, transparent, and people-driven. By playing our parts, we can deliver the estate we all deserve: clean, secure, and thriving with opportunity.
